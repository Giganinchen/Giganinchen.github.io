My Projects

Webiste for house points

During the vacations, I volunteer in a vacation childcare, which takes place every year during the main vacations in my state in Germany. Every week of these vacation games for children there is a theme day. In one of the weeks of the 2023 vacation games, the theme was Harry Potter. It was in previous motto days with the theme that points were assigned by counselors and written down on paper. In a short time of a few hours I created the house points website, which was used the next day during the whole day. Via a separate website, the supervisors were able to assign points for the respective houses. The points were stored in a database so that no progress could be lost. The website automatically updated when a score changed to show the new score. In the end, the website was used until the end of the week, because the competition among the houses was very enjoyable for the children.
